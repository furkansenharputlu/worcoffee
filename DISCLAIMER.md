## 🛑 Are you the owner of a place listed here?

If you are the owner, manager, or authorized representative of a place listed on WorCoffee and you would like the entry to be **removed, corrected, or updated**, we will act quickly and in good faith. You don't need to provide a reason.

Please reach us through any of the following:

- 📷 Instagram DM: [**@furkansnhrptlu**](https://instagram.com/furkansnhrptlu)
- 🐙 Open an issue on GitHub: <https://github.com/furkansenharputlu/worcoffee/issues/new>

Mention the **place name and city**. We aim to respond and act within a few days.
